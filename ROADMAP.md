# Roadmap

Where **MultiScope ATC** is heading. This is a high-level, plain-English view of what we're building and considering.

It is **directional, not a promise** — priorities and timing change, and items may ship, shift, or be dropped. There are no dates here. For what's already shipped, see the [changelog](CHANGELOG.md) and [releases](https://github.com/Dalimk/MultiScope-ATC-Releases/releases).

---

## Current focus — toward a playable demo

We're working toward a first playable demo. The core is in place — schedule-driven traffic, runway allocation, voice control, adjustable weather and ATC frequencies — so the focus now is polish, stability, and clearing the last of the known bugs.

---

## In progress

- **More natural voice control** — more forgiving, more accurate speech recognition.
- **More precise procedure flying** — proper fly-over vs fly-by waypoint behaviour, and cleaner dual-runway departures.
- **Interface polish** — ongoing improvements to controls, settings, the main menu, and data-tag placement.

---

## Planned

- **Guided tutorial** — an approachable introduction for new controllers.
- **Audible traffic and conflict alerts** — not just visual.
- **ACC arrival sequencing** — tools to help build and manage the arrival stream.
- **Smarter, more varied pilot read-backs** — including regional accents.
- **More realistic top-of-descent** — descent planning from cruise level down to the entry points.

---

## Exploring (longer term)

- **Multiplayer** — controlling adjacent sectors together, with deeper Steam integration.
- **Navigraph integration** — real-world navigation data.
- **Diversions** — aircraft diverting when they can't land.
- **VFR traffic.**
- **Sector coordination** between control positions.
